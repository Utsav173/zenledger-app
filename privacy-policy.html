Privacy Policy

LAST UPDATED: FEBRUARY 2026 // SYSTEM V1.1

1. Information We Collect

Temporal prioritizes data minimization. We only collect the minimal structural data necessary to establish an account and synchronize your local application data across devices. The data we sync may include email metadata alongside encrypted representations of your personal financial ledgers (categories, transaction amounts, timestamps, holding structures).

2. Payment Information

Payments for the Temporal PRO subscription are securely managed entirely off-site by our payment provider (e.g., Razorpay, Stripe). Thus, we do not store, process, or ever have access to your full credit card details on our own servers. Any payment processing information collected by Razorpay will be governed strictly by Razorpay's Privacy Policy.

3. Data Usage & Security

The core objective of Temporal is "Local-First". While Convex acts as our synchronization backend to seamlessly back up data between your mobile devices, the data belongs solely to you. We strictly do not sell, share, or broker your personal financial records or telemetry metrics to advertising networks.

4. Analytics

We may optionally collect anonymous crash reports or performance telemetry solely for diagnosing system stability bugs (e.g., React Native crashes) and improving the underlying architecture. This data is fully obfuscated from personal financial identifiers.

5. Your Rights

You may request a complete export of your synchronized data in JSON format, or request complete account deletion which securely drops your Convex user table and immediately wipes all synced transaction data.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding your privacy, data handling, or secure sync mechanisms, contact us directly at utsav@khatriutsav.com or our dedicated security email address.
