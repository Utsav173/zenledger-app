Cancellation & Refund Policy

LAST UPDATED: FEBRUARY 2026 // SYSTEM V1.1

1. The Temporal PRO Advantage

Temporal PRO is offered as a single, one-time lifetime payment rather than a recurring subscription. Upon successful payment processed by Razorpay or Stripe, your account is immediately permanently upgraded to "PRO" status with unlocked access to unlimited transaction tracking, PDF/CSV data exports, and premium biometric vault features.

2. Cancellation Policy

As there are no recurring subscription fees mathematically built into Temporal, there is no need to manually "cancel" a subscription once purchased to avoid future charges. Your purchase is a final, lifetime asset for the current version of the software.

3. Strictly Non-Refundable

Temporal PRO is offered as an instantaneous digital unlock for a nominal one-time fee of ₹49. Because the digital asset is immediately unlocked and processed, all purchases are strictly final and non-refundable. We do not offer a trial period, nor do we offer a refund window. Please review the application features and documentation carefully before upgrading.

4. Dispute Process & Duplicate Charges

While standard purchases are non-refundable, if you were charged twice due to a network or system state error during the Razorpay checkout loop, contact us immediately at utsav@khatriutsav.com with the duplicate receipt records and we will instantly reverse the erroneous secondary transaction.
