# ZenLedger User Manual // v1.0

**Financial Purity.** ZenLedger is a high-performance personal finance tracker designed for the modern stoic. This manual guides you through mastering its minimalist interface to gain complete clarity over your finances.

---

## 1. Getting Started

### Initial Setup

Upon first launch, ZenLedger operates immediately. No lengthy onboarding.

1. **Account**: Secure your data by creating an account.
2. **Sync**: Data synchronizes automatically across devices when online.
3. **Offline Mode**: Full functionality is available without an internet connection. Changes queue and upload once connectivity is restored.

### Dashboard Overview

The **Hub** is your central command center.

- **Sparkline**: The graph at the top visualizes your spending velocity over the month.
- **Month Navigation**: Use the arrows (`←` / `→`) to travel through time.
- **Key Metrics**:
  - **IN**: Total Income
  - **OUT**: Total Expenses
  - **SAVINGS**: Your net savings rate for the period.

---

## 2. Transactions

### Adding an Entry

Tap the **+** button (bottom center) to launch the precision entry mode.

1. **Amount**: Enter the value on the custom numpad.
2. **Type**: Toggle between **Expense** (Red) and **Income** (Green).
3. **Details**:
   - **Category**: Assign a bucket (e.g., Food, Transport).
   - **Note**: Add context (optional).
   - **Date**: Defaults to today; tap to modify.
4. **Save**: Tap the checkmark to commit the entry to the ledger.

### Managing Entries

- **Edit**: Tap any transaction in the list to view its detail card. Tap the **Edit (Pencil)** icon to modify.
- **Delete**: From the detail card, tap the **Delete (Trash)** icon to remove it permanently.

---

## 3. Reports & Analytics

Navigate to the **Reports** tab to unlock financial intelligence.

### Visualizations

- **Spending Breakdown**: A donut chart visualizing expense distribution by category.
- **Top Expense**: Identifies your single largest outgoing transaction for the period.
- **Monthly Trend**: 6-month bar chart comparing total expenses over time.

### Filtering

Use the period selector at the top of the Reports screen to switch views:

- **THIS MONTH**: Current calendar month.
- **LAST 3 MONTHS**: Trailing quarter.
- **LAST 6 MONTHS**: Half-year review.
- **YTD**: Year-to-date performance.

---

## 4. Settings & Configuration

Tailor ZenLedger to your preferences in **Settings**.

### General

- **Currency**: Select your primary currency (USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, INR, etc.). All values automatically format to the chosen locale.
- **Categories**: Manage your expense and income taxonomy.

### Data Management

- **Export Data**: Generate a **CSV** for spreadsheet analysis or a **PDF** report for records.
- **Recovery Kit**: View and save your Backup Codes. **CRITICAL**: Store these safely. They are the only way to recover your account if you lose access to your primary authentication method.

## 5. Security (Pro)

ZenLedger takes data privacy seriously.

### Biometric Lock

Enable **FaceID** or **TouchID** in Settings to require authentication every time the app opens. This ensures your financial data remains viewable only by you, even if your phone is unlocked.
